BIOGRAPHY

Mr. Feng Chen received his B.Eng. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering (ISEE), Zhejiang University (ZJU), Hangzhou, China, in 2015.

Mr. Chen is now pursuing his PhD degree under the supervision of professor Philip K. T. Mok in the Integrated Power Electronics Laboraty (IPEL) at Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering (ECE), The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong SAR, China.

Mr. Chen's research interest involves Power Management IC (PMIC) design and analog and mixed-signal IC design, especially analog and digital linear regulator.
